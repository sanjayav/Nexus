## 1. Executive Summary

The Aeiforo reporting dashboard has a **solid foundation** with GRI and MSX questionnaires, RBAC, evidence library, blockchain anchoring, and analytics. To reach **world-class** and serve **Indian**, **GCC**, and **renewable energy** markets, specific enhancements have been identified and implemented.

| Market | Current Fit | After Enhancements |
|--------|-------------|---------------------|

| **Renewable Energy** | Partial (GHG only) | **Good** — Generation, capacity, RECs, carbon avoidance |

## 5. Renewable Energy Company Compatibility

### Key Metrics for Renewable Energy Companies

| Metric | Purpose | Status |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **Installed capacity (MW)** | Solar, wind, hydro capacity | ✓ Implemented |
| **Renewable generation (MWh)** | Energy produced | ✓ Implemented |
| **Renewable % of total** | Portfolio mix | ✓ Implemented |
| **RECs / certificates** | Renewable Energy Certificates | ✓ Implemented |
| **Carbon avoidance (tCO₂e)** | Displaced fossil fuel emissions | ✓ Implemented |
| **Scope 1 & 2 (GHG)** | Own emissions (often low) | ✓ Already supported |
| **Scope 2 market-based** | RECs, PPAs | ✓ Already supported |

### Renewable Energy Company Fit

- **Solar / Wind / Hydro** generators: Track generation, capacity, RECs, carbon avoidance
- **Low Scope 1** typical; Scope 2 (location-based) often near zero for renewables
- **Market-based** Scope 2 reflects RECs and PPAs
- **BRSR** relevant for Indian renewables (Adani Green, Renew, etc.)
- **GRI 302** (Energy) and **GRI 305** (GHG) fully supported
